# Team Roles & Responsibilities

## Medical Team Lead
- Coordinate with clinical trial sites for patient referrals
- Verify prosthetic eligibility criteria for outreach patients
- Maintain provider credentialing

## Logistics Team Lead
- Manage prosthetic inventory and supply chain
- Coordinate shipping between regional hubs
- Track equipment donations

## Community Outreach Lead
- Schedule and plan outreach events
- Build local partnerships (hospitals, clinics, NGOs)
- Manage volunteer scheduling in each region

## Regional Coordinators
- **Chicago**: Midwest hub coordinator
- **Boston**: Northeast hub coordinator
- **Atlanta**: Southeast hub coordinator
