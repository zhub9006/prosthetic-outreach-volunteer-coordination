# Meeting Logistics — Coordinator Kickoff

## Proposed Meeting Location: Nashville, TN

Nashville provides a geographic midpoint between the three regional teams:

| Team | City | Distance to Nashville |
|------|------|----------------------|
| Midwest | Chicago, IL | ~550 miles |
| Northeast | Boston, MA | ~1,150 miles |
| Southeast | Atlanta, GA | ~250 miles |

### Why Nashville?
- Centrally located along I-65 corridor, accessible from all three regions
- Major airport (BNA) with direct flights to Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta
- Abundance of meeting venues: cafes, coworking spaces, restaurants
- Affordable lodging and dining options

### Recommended Venue Types
1. **Cafes** — Casual, collaborative atmosphere
2. **Coworking Spaces** — Professional, AV-equipped (WeWork, Galvanize)
3. **Restaurant Private Rooms** — Group dining + meeting hybrid
4. **Libraries** — Free, quiet, professional (Main Branch Nashville Public Library)

### Action Items

- [ ] Book venue at least 4 weeks in advance
- [ ] Arrange travel reimbursements
- [ ] Prepare agendum and pre-reading materials
- [ ] Follow up after meeting with action items and next-steps

### Venue Suggestions (Downtown Nashville)

- **WeWork Nashville** — 150 Printers Alley, professional coworking
- **Broadway area cafes** — Multiple options along Broadway for informal meetups
